Tile Recipe
=================
1. Create wall outline, solid black, inset 1/8 grid (25px)
2. Draw walls using Stone Wall 2 brush at 100px
3. Structure Layer Group
   - Black (25px contract > 25px feather > delete x2)
   - Foreground (10px feather > delete x5 > apply pattern overlay)
   - Walls Layer
   - Wall Shadow (apply drop shadow 50%/25px at 75% alpha)
